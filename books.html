Books / separate sequences

The books do not share one numbering system.

The Long Reverb is the larger creative container. The Hidden Current studies and the Bardos fiction remain distinct series, with their own order, visual language, and promises to the reader.

These pages establish the current architecture without presenting unfinished manuscripts as released products.

The Hidden Current series

Resonance, space, and the structures beneath hearing.

Nonfiction and field inquiry. Blue-gold resonance, sacred geometry, acoustic architecture, psychoacoustics, and the lived consequences of sound. Radio-dial imagery does not define this series.

Hidden Current / 01

The Hidden Current

Sound, space, and the architecture of resonance. A field book about the forces carried through rooms, memory, ritual, recording, and attention.

The Architecture of Resonance

The Bardos series

Where grief becomes transmission.

Fiction and autofiction under analog pressure: black, aged brass, amber light, tape, institutional rooms, shortwave language, memory failures, and recognition. The work remains spare, haunted, and exact.

Bardos / 01

Bardo Conditions

A novel built from grief, signal, recognition, administrative violence, and the unstable border between what was lost and what continues to answer.
