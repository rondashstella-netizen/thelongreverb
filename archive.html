Archive / opening in stages

What remains after the signal passes.

The archive is not a storage closet. It is the public evidence layer of The Long Reverb: images, ledgers, rooms, recordings, witness, fragments, and the structures that connect them.

Nothing enters the public archive merely because it exists. Rights, continuity, provenance, and project placement are checked before release.

Archive law

Provenance before velocity.

Originals remain preserved. Derivatives retain parent records. Experimental renders do not become canonical by accident. Incorrect imprint language is corrected before public use. Personal and third-party material remains gated until permissions are clear.

The archive does not prove that the past is stable. It proves that somebody kept listening.
