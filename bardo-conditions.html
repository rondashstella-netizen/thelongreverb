Bardo Conditions

A novel built from grief, signal, recognition, administrative violence, and the unstable border between what was lost and what continues to answer.

The book has a defined manuscript and production lane. Public preview, store placement, and release remain behind final approval.

Production staged · release gated
Field

Where grief becomes transmission.

The book moves through institutional rooms, unstable records, recognition events, damaged authority, and the pressure of a signal that refuses to disappear merely because a system cannot classify it.

Visual language

Black field. Aged brass. Amber transport light.

Tape, reel-to-reel machinery, shortwave pressure, static, shadow, and analog residue belong to this book’s visual world. Final art enters only from approved master assets.

Series law

The first Bardos book.

Bardo Conditions is Bardos #1. It is not Book II of a single Long Reverb sequence, and it is not cross-numbered with The Hidden Current nonfiction series.
