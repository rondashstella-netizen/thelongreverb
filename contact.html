Send the signal to the right room.

Use the route below for publishing, archive, rights, witness, collaboration, press, and serious project inquiries.

This address is the current fallback route for The Long Reverb and Signal Tower Press until dedicated project mailboxes are independently verified.

Publishing

Books, excerpts, rights

Identify the work, intended use, territory, format, timing, and whether the request concerns text, art, audio, or an edition.

Witness

Archive contributions

State what you witnessed or preserved, when it occurred, what materials exist, who owns them, and whether public attribution is permitted.

Collaboration

Sound, image, systems

Describe the actual project, the role being requested, available budget or exchange, decision authority, and the next concrete step.

Boundary

Do not send private credentials or proprietary production files.

Initial contact should contain enough context to route the request, not sensitive account access, unpublished full manuscripts, private family material, or protected spatial-audio system details.

A useful message names the artifact, the request, the authority, and the next move.
